NotesCraft AI is a smart tool that converts your PDFs into structured, editable study notes in seconds, helping students and professionals save time and learn efficiently.

How should I use NotesCraft?

Simply upload your PDF, and NotesCraft will automatically process it to generate clear and editable notes in Word format. No complicated steps required!

During Early Access, NotesCraft is completely free for a limited time. After the full launch, it will become a paid product for everyone.

What problem does NotesCraft solve?

Many AI note-making tools skip important topics, summarize content too much, or struggle with large documents. NotesCraft processes entire PDFs while keeping context intact, delivering clear, complete, and editable notes for easier studying.
